Pancakes

Description of Pancakes

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish made from a simple batter and cooked on a griddle or frying pan. They are soft, fluffy, and can be served with a variety of toppings such as syrup, fruits, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Cooking Procedure

  • Mix Dry Ingredients:In a large bowl, combine 1 cup of flour, 2 tablespoons of sugar, 2 teaspoons of baking powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Mix well.
  • Combine Wet Ingredients:In another bowl, whisk together 1 cup of milk, 1 large egg, 2 tablespoons of melted butter or vegetable oil, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (if using).
  • Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients:Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined; the batter should be slightly lumpy.
  • Preheat Griddle:Preheat a griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  • Cook Pancakes:

    Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.

    Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.

  • Serve:Serve the pancakes warm with your choice of toppings such as syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or butter.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pancakes!
